Hey, I'm Nic 🖖

I'm a Marketing Specialist at a South African marketing agency, with a deep focus on performance marketing and Google Ads. But I didn't start there.

I've been around the block in the marketing space — starting off as a copywriter, moving into web development, dabbling in conversion rate optimization, going deep into email marketing, and finally finding my groove in performance marketing.

Every step taught me something new. And now I'm here, writing about all of it.
