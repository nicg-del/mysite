Hey, I'm Nic 🖖

I'm a performance marketer based in South Africa, specialising in Google Ads and Meta Ads. I help businesses grow through paid media — from strategy and setup all the way to optimisation and reporting.

I didn't start in performance. I've been around the block — starting as a copywriter, moving into web development, dabbling in CRO, going deep into email marketing, and eventually finding my home in paid media.

Every step taught me something the next role needed. That cross-disciplinary background is one of the things I'm most proud of.

AI & automation

These days I'm increasingly obsessed with how AI can be used to build smarter workflows, tools, and systems that make marketers move faster. Whether that's automating reporting, building custom GPT tools for campaign analysis, or just finding clever ways to remove the boring stuff — I'm into it.

Google Tag Manager is also a big part of my world. Proper tracking is the foundation of any good performance campaign, and most people underestimate how much it matters.

Why this site?

Have you ever stumbled across a blog post and thought "I'm bookmarking this" because of how much value it packs? That's the goal. An honest, no-fluff space where I share what I'm learning — about performance marketing, AI, GTM, and everything in between.
