Hey, I'm Nic 🖖

I'm a performance marketer based in South Africa, specialising in Google Ads and Meta Ads. I help businesses grow through paid media — from strategy and setup to optimisation and reporting.

I've been around the block in the marketing space — starting off as a copywriter, moving into web development, dabbling in conversion rate optimisation, going deep into email marketing, and eventually finding my home in performance marketing.

These days I'm equally obsessed with Google Tag Manager and how AI can be used to build smarter workflows, tools, and systems that make marketers faster and more effective.

Why this site?

Have you ever stumbled across a blog post and thought "f*ck, I'm bookmarking this" because of how much value it provides? That's the goal. An open diary where I share what I'm learning about performance marketing, AI, GTM, and everything in between — honestly and without fluff.

Beyond work

When I'm not knee-deep in campaign dashboards or building AI automations, you'll find me with a good glass of wine, exploring big ideas, or just enjoying this weird and wonderful corner of the internet I've built here.
